Which at-home hair removal device should you buy?

This tool compares every major at-home IPL and laser hair removal device on the specs that actually matter: fluence (energy per pulse), wavelength, skin tone compatibility, treatment frequency, and lifetime flash count. All data is sourced from manufacturer specifications and hands-on testing at Science Over Fluff.

IPL vs. laser: IPL devices are affordable and widely available but are limited to Fitzpatrick I–III and dark hair. Diode laser and LED devices deliver significantly higher fluence and are the only safe option for Fitzpatrick IV–VI skin. If you're unsure, use the quiz in the header.
